Aymeric Laporte Returns to Athletic Club

Laporte has completed a free transfer move from Al Hilal back to his boyhood club, Athletic Bilbao.

Date: September 2025

Aymeric Laporte has officially returned to Athletic Club, sealing an emotional free transfer move from Saudi Arabian side Al Hilal back to the Basque Country.

The Spain international first broke into senior football at Bilbao in 2012 before moving to Manchester City in 2018, where he won multiple Premier League titles. After a stint at Al Hilal, Laporte is back home, signing a three-year contract.

Laporte said: “This is home for me. I want to give everything for this team again and make the fans proud.”

Athletic Club president Jon Uriarte called the move “a statement of intent,” believing Laporte’s leadership will boost their European qualification hopes.

Harvey Elliott Joins Aston Villa on Loan

Liverpool’s midfielder Harvey Elliott has secured a one-year loan to Aston Villa.

Date: September 2025

Elliott, regarded as one of England’s brightest young talents, will spend the 2025/26 season on loan at Aston Villa. The move is seen as an opportunity for him to gain more first-team minutes.

Aston Villa coach Unai Emery welcomed the signing, saying Elliott’s creativity will be key to their attacking play.

Barcelona Confirm New Stadium Opening

The renovated Camp Nou will officially reopen this month after years of construction.

Date: September 2025

Barcelona has confirmed the reopening of the renovated Camp Nou, a project that cost over €1.5 billion. The stadium now has a capacity of 105,000, with state-of-the-art facilities.

Club president Joan Laporta called it “the start of a new era for Barça,” with a grand ceremony planned ahead of their La Liga matchday.

UEFA Nations League Fixtures Released

UEFA has confirmed the full schedule for the 2025/26 Nations League campaign.

Date: September 2025

The UEFA Nations League draw has produced several exciting clashes, with France set to face Germany, while England will meet Spain in the group stage.

The competition kicks off in October 2025, with the finals scheduled for summer 2026.
